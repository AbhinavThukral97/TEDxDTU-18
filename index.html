About

In the spirit of “ideas worth spreading,” TED created TEDx, a program of local, self-organised, independent events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, a collection of TED Talk videos and live speakers combine to spark deep discussions and connections.

Speakers

TEDxDTU 2018 Speakers

The organising team put together a revolutionary line-up of speakers consisting of artists, celebrities, industrialists, visionaries and motivators who inspired thousands of people with their words of change. Some of our past speakers include Dilip Chhabra (founder of DC Design), Richa Chadda (a dynamic Bollywood actress, a recipient of the Filmfare Award), Monica Dogra (a versatile indie American musician and actress), Aaron Friedland (founder and the executive director of the Walking School Bus, that supports access to education) & many more inspiring visionaries.
