About TED

TED is a global platform with an annual conference that brings together the world’s leading thinkers, social entrepreneurs and change makers to share ideas that matter in any discipline—technology, entertainment, design, science, environment, business. The talks at the conference, “TED Talks,” are then made available for free online at TED.com. Over 100 million people view TED videos.

About TEDx

In the spirit of “ideas worth spreading,” TED created TEDx, a program of local, self-organised, independent events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, a collection of TED Talk videos and live speakers combine to spark deep discussions and connections.

About Delhi Technological University

Established in the year 1941 as Delhi Polytechnic, Delhi Technological University is the mother institute of a number of national institutes, including Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology, School of Planning and Architecture, Delhi, College of Art, Delhi and Faculty of Management Studies. Housing some of the brilliant minds in this country, lakhs of students compete every year to get admission to this coveted institution. The graduates from this institute are now pioneers in their field.

Our Event– TEDxDTU

Re-established in the year 2015, our team has worked tirelessly to deliver one of the highest quality TEDx events in India. Our previous event, held on the 18th of March 2017, was witnessed by an audience of 150+, consisting of designers, entrepreneurs, professionals, technology enthusiasts and motivated students alike; the top faculty of Delhi Technological University and representatives from various media organisations.

The organising team put together a revolutionary line-up of speakers consisting of artists, celebrities, industrialists, visionaries and motivators who inspired thousands of people with their words of change.
